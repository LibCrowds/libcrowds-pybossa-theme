About

LibCrowds, in association with BL Labs, is a British Library crowd-sourcing experiment focused on making important research materials at the British Library more readily available to a wider audience.

{{ _('Current Project: Convert-a-Card') }}

In our first project series, Convert-a-Card, we're focusing on liberating collection items discoverable only through our physical card catalogues onsite.

While the British Library is relatively young for a national library, much of its vast historical collections were acquired before computers were used for cataloguing as standard. So while the library's online catalogue Explore provides public access to nearly 57 million records, there are thousands of important research items that can still only be found by searching the old physical card catalogues onsite.

Records matched, located, transcribed or translated as part of Convert-a-Card will be uploaded to the British Library's Explore catalogue for anyone to search online. By participating you will have a direct impact on the availability of research material to anyone interested in the diverse collections available at the British Library.
