{{ _('About') }}

{{ brand }} is a crowd-sourcing platform with the ultimate goal of making important research material more readily available to a wider audience.

Each project at {{ brand }} contains a collection of tasks related to a specific area of research. These tasks require human intelligence and are not things that can be accurately processed by computers. While each individual task can usually be completed quickly, the collections of tasks are often vast. The only feasible way to complete such a number of tasks within a reasonable length of time is via widespread user contributions.

By participating you will have a direct impact on helping future research by anyone interested in the diverse collections available at the British Library.

{{ _('Technology') }}

{{ brand }} is built on top of PyBossa , an open-source framework for crowd-sourcing projects. Individual applications are written in HTML and Javascript, with some also making use of PHP.
