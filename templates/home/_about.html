About

{{brand}}, in association with BL Labs, is an experimental crowd-sourcing platform focused on making important research materials held at the British Library more readily available to a wider audience. By participating you will have a direct impact on future research conducted using these diverse collections.

Current Project: Convert-a-Card

In our first project series, Convert-a-Card, we're focusing on liberating collection items currently only discoverable onsite, through our physical card catalogues.

While the British Library is relatively young for a national library, much of its vast historical collections were acquired long before computers were used for cataloguing as standard. So, while the library's online catalogue, Explore, provides public access to nearly 57 million records, there are still thousands of important research items that can only be found by searching the old physical card catalogues.

Records matched, located, transcribed or translated as part of Convert-a-Card will be uploaded to the British Library's Explore catalogue for anyone to search online. By participating you will have a direct impact on the availability of research material to anyone interested in the diverse collections available at the British Library.

{{ _('Technology') }}

{{ brand }} is built on top of PyBossa , an open-source Python framework for the creation of crowd-sourcing projects. To complement the PyBossa framework, a custom Flask plugin is used to handle certain {{brand}} specific features. The {{brand}} theme is written primarily in HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript, as are the individual crowd-sourcing projects. Both the theme and plugin are open-source and any suggestions or contributions are welcome.
